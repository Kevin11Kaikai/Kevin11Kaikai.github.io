Intelligent Table Tennis Coach

Recent decades have witnessed the growing popularity of table tennis in the United States. However, it is so expensive to hire a table tennis coach that many players cannot afford the fee. Therefore, we propose our Intelligent Table Tennis Coach [1] to teach people how to play table tennis, at a relatively low cost, from consistency, framework, timing, and acceleration. Consistency and framework are determined by poses extracted by mediapipe [2], timing is measured by detecting the ball's direction through color recognition based on OpenCV [3], and the system can get the acceleration from ADXL345. Overall, our intelligent table tennis coach, an alternative to the human coach, can give an evaluation of a table tennis player from 4 aspects and provide them with feedback that can help them make progress.

Motivation

Based on our research, the demand for table tennis coaching is huge in USA, while the fee is extremely high. In order to save the cost, we would like to design a system that is easy to implement in practice. And also, we aim to combine both IoT knowledge that we have learned in class and our interests toward table tennis to help amateur table tennis players improve themselves.
